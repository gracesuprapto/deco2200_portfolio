Process

User Research

My group divided up different methods so that we could gain a deeper and richer understanding of our user through various promptings and ways in which they would be understood. I chose research methods that covered the attitudinal and behavioural, and qualitative and quantitative categories to ensure that the data I collected was directly specific to the potential user.

Concept Designs and Storyboarding

Previous Next

The 3 final concepts we came up with included a more defined solution to the autonomous aspect of the design brief. We also drew up rough storyboards that explained the concepts in context and how the user would approach and interact with each concept. This allowed us to see the flow of the user and how they would interact from one point to another.

Testing

Low fidelity prototyping allows for our concept to come to life. We chose to use paper as it is a quick and easy way to create and adjust our designs so that our users can test with a physical prototype with similar feel to it. This way, it allows them to envision what the actual interface of the app will look like.

We gathered users from different backgrounds and experiences of both tours and the systems. This allows us to see from different perspectives so that we are able to really test and get a feel of what our app will be like when it’s functioning.

Iterated and Tested

The design proven by testing and fit to our user's specifications, having been developed and iterated through multiple rounds of testing. User testing revealed elements that disrupted the flow of navigation. We were able to iterate based on feedback and improve these aspects.

User testing methods used to research the experience include:

  • Think Aloud
  • Cognitive Walkthroughs
  • Interviews
  • A/B Testing
  • Surveys & Questionnaires

The wireframes were designed on Sketch. This tool allowed our designs to be created with ease and allow us to change any thing that needed to be adjusted from our feedback from user testing. We then used inVision to create the mockups for users to test it on their hand held devices.
